the best Engineering colleges affiliated with AKTU in UP

The Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology was founded in 1997 and is managed by the Meerut, Uttar Pradesh Educational & Social Welfare Society.
MIET college is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education, Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, New Delhi, and is affiliated with Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam University- AKTU (previously Uttar Pradesh Technical University.
